According to the Christian calendar, the Christmas season began at sunset on Christmas Eve and runs for 12 days (you know, “The Twelve Days of Christmas”). That means you should crank up that Christmas music and [listen proudly](https://www.thegospelcoalition.org/article/turn-off-christmas-music/) until January 6!

If you need festive music suggestions, I’ve got you covered. For my annual Christmas playlist (listen on [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/5SI2vC8Cql1mgcocvkQrlJ?si=4a7c34ca666443d3) or [Apple Music](https://music.apple.com/us/playlist/nostalgic-christmas/pl.u-BNA66J3u2kBgD)), I went with a vintage theme, putting together 75 nostalgic Christmas recordings your parents or grandparents might have listened to. I hope you and your family can listen to this playlist together and savor the joy and memories of this sacred season. You’ll see the track list below.
