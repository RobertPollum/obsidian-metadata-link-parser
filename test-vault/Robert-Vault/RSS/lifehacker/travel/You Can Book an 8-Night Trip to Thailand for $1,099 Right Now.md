Beautiful beaches, delicious—yet cheap!—food, dense jungles, and breathtaking Buddhist temples are just a few of the things that made my Thailand trip such a great time. If you’ve been priced out of visiting Thailand in the past, this could be the moment for you to experience what the country has to offer. Groupon is offering a [10-day (eight-night) trip to Thailand](https://www.groupon.com/deals/ga-affordable-world-tours-bangkok-krabi-phuket-13 "open in a new window") complete with roundtrip flights, hotels, daily breakfast, admission to sightseeing locations, and an English-speaking tour guide starting at $1,099 for those who book by Aug. 31, 2023.  

## How does the deal work?

The Groupon is from [Affordable World](https://www.affordableworld.com/ "open in a new window"), an online budget-vacation travel agency that makes packages for international trips. Affordable World is currently having a sale on its Thailand package for trips taken in 2023. But Groupon has the hook-up with even more discounts if you buy the packages through them, including for dates in 2024. You need to pick whether you’re going to take off from New York City (JFK) or Los Angeles (LAX). Then you need to pick from two package options, both for the same amount of days but different locations within Thailand. Finally, you need to nail down your departure date. Depending on your previous choices, you will see different prices on packages for different dates. At the time of this writing, there are two package options for $1,099 from LAX, both leaving late November 2023 and two other ones from JFK for Nov. 30, 2023, at the same price (multiple people can buy the same package, so there should still be plenty of Groupons to go around). Outside of that, there are a number of dates and packages available for $1,199.

None of these activities will be included, but you’ll have the option to get them once you’re there if you’d like.

## Some important details

The Groupon is only applicable as advertised if two people buy one each. This means you need to take someone with you who you’ll be sharing the hotel with, so they need to purchase the same package as you for you to get that price. Otherwise, you will get charged some hefty fees to go solo.

Keep in mind you will need a passport, and it must be valid for at least six months _after_ the date of travel. Depending on your airline, you might have some baggage fees. If you have more questions about this deal, you can call Affordable World at (888) 737-2742.
