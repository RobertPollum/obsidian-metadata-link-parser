Veterans can get quite a few deals on Veterans Day this Saturday, Nov. 11, but you have to know what they are in order to secure them. Here are a few places where veterans can get discounts or special treatment in honor of the holiday. Just be sure to have your military ID or VA discount card on you.

## Veterans Day food discounts

Active-duty military members, vets, reservists, and National Guard members get a free full-sized entree from a special menu at [Applebee’s](https://www.applebees.com/en/veterans-day-free-meals "open in a new window") on Nov. 11, plus a $5 card to redeem on dine-in or to-go orders for three weeks. Active-duty members and veterans can also get a free meal from a select menu at [Chili’s](https://www.chilis.com/restaurant-events/veterans-day "open in a new window") on the big day.

[Benihana](https://www.military.com/discounts/benihana-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") offers active-duty military members and veterans a free appetizer with any lunch or dinner entree on Nov. 11.

[Dave & Busters](https://www.daveandbusters.com/us/en/holiday?gclid=EAIaIQobChMI8LOOhf60ggMVjUxHAR2KZQW9EAAYASAAEgLUl_D_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds "open in a new window") is offering veterans a free entree (up to $20) plus a free $10 Power Card on November 11.

[Buffalo Wild Wings](https://www.military.com/discounts/buffalo-wild-wings-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") will give 10 free boneless wings (plus fries) to vets and active-duty service members on Nov. 11, but only if dining in or walk-in ordering. [Hooters](https://www.hooters.com/about/news/hooters-celebrates-veterans-day-by-serving-up-free-meals-to-military-heroes "open in a new window") is also offering a free entree from a special menu for veterans on November 11.

Military members, vets, and their spouses get a free small hot brewed coffee of the day or cold press at [Caribou Coffee](https://www.military.com/discounts/caribou-coffee-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") on Nov. 11. You can also get a free coffee or fountain drink at [Casey’s General Stores](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20231101873006/en/Casey%E2%80%99s-and-Pepsi%C2%AE-Honor-Military-Veterans-and-Their-Families-with-Annual-Giving-Campaign "open in a new window"), a free donut at [Dunkin](https://www.military.com/discounts/dunkin-donuts-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window")’, and a free kolache and cup of coffee at [Kolache Factory](https://www.qsrmagazine.com/news/kolache-factory-to-offer-free-breakfast-to-military-on-veterans-day/ "open in a new window"). (Kolache Factory’s special is only running in the morning.)

[Golden Corral](https://www.dav.org/learn-more/news/2023/2023-military-appreciation-night/ "open in a new window")’s Military Appreciation Night is happening on Nov. 13 from 5 p,m. to close, and includes a free meal when dining in.

Hit [Denny’s](https://www.dennys.com/news/americas-diner-honors-americas-heroes-dennys-celebrate-military-personnel-free-original-grand "open in a new window") on Nov. 10 for a free Original Grand Slam from 5 a.m. to noon.

[IHOP](https://www.hooters.com/about/news/hooters-celebrates-veterans-day-by-serving-up-free-meals-to-military-heroes "open in a new window") will give vets and active-duty personnel free Red, White, & Blueberry Pancakes or a pancake combo on Nov. 11.

Nab a free Bloomin’ Onion and beverage at [Outback Steakhouse](https://www.outback.com/offers/military-mates "open in a new window") on Nov. 11 if you purchase an adult entree, though veterans and service members get 10% off their entire check every day of the year.

[Playa Bowls](https://www.military.com/discounts/playa-bowls-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") is offering a free bowl with military ID on Nov. 11 and [Smoothie King](https://www.qsrmagazine.com/news/smoothie-king-to-offer-free-beverages-to-military-personnel-on-veterans-day/ "open in a new window") is offering a free red, white, or blue smoothie from a special menu.

## **Veterans Day retail discounts**

[Adidas](https://www.military.com/discounts/adidas-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") gives vets and active-duty military members 40% on its website until Nov.12 this year.

[Academy Sports + Outdoors](https://www.military.com/discounts/academy-sports-outdoors-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") offers 10% off to active-duty service members and vets from Oct. 22 to Nov. 12, both online and in-store.

Get a free Car Care Check at [Goodyear](https://corporate.goodyear.com/us/en/media/news/goodyear-offers-free-veterans-day-car-care-services-and-tire-discounts-for-those-who-served-and-continue-to-serve-our-country.html "open in a new window") if you’re active-duty or a veteran. You can schedule your appointments between Nov. 10 and 14, then redeem the service before Nov. 19.

Vets, active and former military personnel, _and_ their immediate families get 30% off in-store purchases at [Kohl’s](https://corporate.kohls.com/news/archive-/2023/november/kohl-s-celebrates-veterans-day- "open in a new window") from Nov. 11 to 13.

You’ll need [this coupon](https://media.officedepot.com/image/upload/v1698263299/CREATIVE/CREATIVE_2023/SITE/WWW/Marketing/RET-161850.23_Veterans_Day_Flyer_10.17.23.pdf "open in a new window") and to be a vet, active-duty personnel, a reservist, or a dependent of one to get 25% off at Office Depot and Office Max from Nov. 11 through 13.

[Target’s](https://corporate.target.com/press/fact-sheet/2023/10/target-is-again-thanking-military-families-with-a-discount-for-veterans-day "open in a new window") 10% military discount runs from Oct. 29 to Nov. 11 and is available to active-duty military personnel, vets, and their families. It works online and in-store and can be applied to two separate transactions.

[Tractor Supply](https://ir.tractorsupply.com/newsroom/news-releases/news-releases-details/2023/Tractor-Supply-Honors-Military-Veterans-and-Active-Service-Members-With-Veterans-Day-Discount/default.aspx "open in a new window") is offering a 15% discount to active-duty military members, veterans, and their dependents on Nov. 11.

Veterans can get 25% off their in-store purchases at [Vitamin Shoppe](https://www.military.com/discounts/vitamin-shoppe-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") and Super Supplements from Nov. 11 to 13.

[Walgreens](https://news.walgreens.com/press-center/walgreens-honors-veterans-active-duty-military-and-families-with-veterans-day-discount-friday-november-10-through-monday-november-13.htm "open in a new window") and Duane Reade are offering 20% off eligible items from November 10 to 13 if you’re active-duty, a veteran, or a family member of one. [Rite Aid](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20231102834129/en/Rite-Aid-to-Honor-Military-Service-Members-with-Veteran-Appreciation-Discount "open in a new window") offers 15% off to customers with both a valid military ID and Rite Aid Rewards program membership from Nov. 10 to 12.

At [IKEA](https://www.ikea.com/us/en/stores/events/ikea-college-park-md/celebrate-veterans-day-at-ikea--014da8ef-acf0-4755-b241-f6bdb5c4f08b/ "open in a new window"), veterans and active-duty military members with a valid ID get 15% off on in-store home furnishing purchases (up to $3,000) between Nov. 10 and 22.

## **Veterans Day personal care discounts**

[Great Clips](https://www.greatclips.com/about-us/giving/thank-a-veteran "open in a new window") is offering a free haircut and free haircut card for future use to any veterans or active service members on Nov. 11. Non-military customers can also grab a free haircut card to give to active-duty or veteran pals that day. The cards are redeemable Nov. 12 through December 1.

## **Veterans Day entertainment and travel discounts**

[La Quinta by Wyndham](https://www.wyndhamhotels.com/laquinta/hotel-deals/military "open in a new window") is offering 12% off their Best Available Rate to eligible military members.  

Members of the military and veterans can get 15% off of bookings and stays at [Red Roof](https://www.military.com/discounts/red-roof-inn-veterans-day-military-discount "open in a new window") from Nov. 1 to Dec. 30. Use code VP 623095.
