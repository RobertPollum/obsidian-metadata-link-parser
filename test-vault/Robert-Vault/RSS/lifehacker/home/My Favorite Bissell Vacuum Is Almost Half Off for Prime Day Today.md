If you knew me in person, you might not necessarily guess that I have a favorite vacuum. Nothing about me suggests that I spend a lot of time being fussy over a cleaning brand. But cleaning less often than I should is precisely why I have a favorite vacuum: I may not tidy up often enough, but when I do, I need one that works. And in my tiny Manhattan apartment, I find the space for not one, but two Bissell vacuums. (One is for what I call "the horrors," and the other is for normal cleaning.) Regardless of how you live, you may also be considering a new vacuum, and these Bissell models are on sale for Prime Day.

## My beloved Bissell CrossWave is nearly half off

I love my [CrossWave OmniForce](https://zdcs.link/911Xdk?pageview_type=RSS&template=content&module=content_body&element=offer&item=text-link&element_label=CrossWave%20OmniForce&short_url=911Xdk&u=https%3A%2F%2Flifehacker.com%2Ffeed%2Frss%2Fhome "open in a new window"), which [I've evangelized about frequently](https://lifehacker.com/home/best-cleaning-swaps-ive-made). It's both a vacuum and a mop, allowing you to use it on both hard and soft floors. The cleaning solution gets dispensed, the brush spreads it around, and the vacuum sucks it back up into a separate chamber. It's ideal for both the squeaky-clean person (maybe you) and the desperately lazy (definitely me).
