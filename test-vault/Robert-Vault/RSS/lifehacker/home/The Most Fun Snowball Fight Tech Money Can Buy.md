Winter weather can be miserable, but there is one huge perk: snowball fights. Any time a deep carpet of snow gets dumped on us, you can count on a bunch of kids (or young-at-heart adults) engaging in epic snowball fights, sometimes involving surprisingly well-engineered snow forts and sophisticated tactics.

This is the 21st century, however—if you’re still fighting your snowball fights with nothing but your bare hands and a sense of determination, you’re missing out. We can’t all be Buddy the Elf, [shotgunning perfectly made snowballs](https://youtu.be/Ao_mMCOehU4?si=6jDwhNeyAAPCEBB5 "open in a new window") like some sort of winter weather Terminator—but we _can_ invest in some of the most fun snowball tech money can buy. If you’re looking to dominate your neighborhood’s winter wars (or put some snowball-tossing whippersnappers in their place), here’s what to buy.

If you don’t _have_ a few minutes because you’re already under attack, you might turn to this [5-in-1 snowball maker](https://zdcs.link/922Lv1?pageview_type=RSS&template=content&module=content_body&element=offer&item=text-link&element_label=5-in-1%20snowball%20maker&short_url=922Lv1&u=https%3A%2F%2Flifehacker.com%2Ffeed%2Frss%2Fhome "open in a new window") that will let you quickly amass some serious firepower.

If you want to get a little more whimsical, you can also pick up snowball-making sets that include [other shapes](https://zdcs.link/znBkeD?pageview_type=RSS&template=content&module=content_body&element=offer&item=text-link&element_label=other%20shapes&short_url=znBkeD&u=https%3A%2F%2Flifehacker.com%2Ffeed%2Frss%2Fhome "open in a new window"), like ducks or hearts, if that’s your jam.

Once you have your snowballs assembled, you’ll need something to augment your throwing power. They also come with built-in snowball makers, so when you run out of your initial supply, you can quickly make and deploy fresh snowballs without missing a beat.

## Defense

No one wants a snowball to the face, so having a [snowball shield](https://zdcs.link/av8GRW?pageview_type=RSS&template=content&module=content_body&element=offer&item=text-link&element_label=snowball%20shield&short_url=av8GRW&u=https%3A%2F%2Flifehacker.com%2Ffeed%2Frss%2Fhome "open in a new window") on hand can keep you dry and unscathed while you rain snowballs on your opponents. Of course, this doesn’t have to be a snowball-specific shield—just about [any plastic shield](https://zdcs.link/9YRY14?pageview_type=RSS&template=content&module=content_body&element=offer&item=text-link&element_label=any%20plastic%20shield&short_url=9YRY14&u=https%3A%2F%2Flifehacker.com%2Ffeed%2Frss%2Fhome "open in a new window") will help protect you from the snowballs being thrown your way.

## Bonus: Indoor

Itching to have a snowball fight, but you live in a warm-weather area that hasn’t seen snow in decades? Or there just hasn’t been enough snowfall? Or it’s summer? Get yourself an [indoor snowball fight kit](https://zdcs.link/9l8jOE?pageview_type=RSS&template=content&module=content_body&element=offer&item=text-link&element_label=indoor%20snowball%20fight%20kit&short_url=9l8jOE&u=https%3A%2F%2Flifehacker.com%2Ffeed%2Frss%2Fhome "open in a new window") and go to town. While you won’t get the visceral joy of smashing snow into someone’s head from a distance, you can replicate just about everything else that’s fun about snowball fights without the cold, damp, or, you know, _snow_. Throw in some couch-cushion forts and you have a guaranteed good time.
