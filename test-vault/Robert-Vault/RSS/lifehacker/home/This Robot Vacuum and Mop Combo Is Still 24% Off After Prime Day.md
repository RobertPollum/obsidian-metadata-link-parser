The Deebot X8 Pro Omni delivers 18,000Pa suction, hot water mop washing with hot air drying, and a mop extension mechanism that reaches corners more effectively than side brushes. Instead of spinning pads, it uses a mop resembling a paint roller that continuously cleans itself, so you don’t have to worry about dirty streaks being spread around your home. [PCMag said the robot cleaner is](https://www.pcmag.com/reviews/ecovacs-deebot-x8-pro-omni "open in a new window") “Outstanding," praising the roller brush and its long-lasting battery life, which can exceed two hours on a single charge (though runtime may shorten when using heavy suction and wet mopping).

For obstacle avoidance, the Deebot X8 Pro Omni can lift itself over thresholds as high as 0.79 inches. It uses embedded LiDAR (which fits better under low-profile furniture), a built-in camera, and 3D sensors for smarter navigation and obstacle avoidance. You can schedule, adjust settings, and customize mop/vacuum configurations on the app, which offers extensive customization options. According to PCMag, it can map a multi-level, 1,500-square-foot apartment in under eight minutes. The dock is also equipped with automated features, including a self-emptying dustbin, mop washing, and water refill.
