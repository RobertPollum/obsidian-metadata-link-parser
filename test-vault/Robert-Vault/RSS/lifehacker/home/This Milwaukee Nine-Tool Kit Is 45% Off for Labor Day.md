## Why Milwaukee is a good brand

Milwaukee has a more than 100-year history of producing power tools, with their first portable power drill being produced for the Ford motor company assembly line in 1924. They came out with their first line of cordless tools in the 1990s and have since become a favorite to pros and hobbyists alike for the durability of their tools.

I’ve been using their tools for more than a decade in my personal tool kit, and in addition to them holding up to a lot of use and abuse, the line of tools that the current battery system is compatible with includes more than 300 different tools including landscaping, woodworking, and metalworking tools. This means that with my set of batteries, I can get tools for many different jobs without needing to invest in a different set of batteries. Investing in a brand of cordless tools that can accommodate many different tools with their batteries can save money on the need for multiple battery sets, and you can explore new interests and expand your skill set without spending money on more batteries and chargers.
