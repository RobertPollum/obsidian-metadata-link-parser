**Las Vegas, NV** — President Donald J. Trump’s transition team co-chairs Tulsi Gabbard and Robert F. Kennedy Jr. hosted a special event with Nevada voters on Saturday to discuss Trump’s plan to address serious issues facing local residents and the nation. The conversation was moderated by NASCAR legend Danica Patrick with a Las Vegas audience at maximum capacity along Team Trump’s _**Reclaim America Tour.**_  
  
Gabbard and Kennedy shared how the Democratic Party they joined decades ago is no more and what led them to endorsing President Trump. “The fact that President Trump is welcoming people like Bobby Kennedy and myself, and so many others from across all party lines, shows us what kind of administration he will have and what kind of leadership he will bring,” **said former Democrat Congresswoman Tulsi Gabbard, now an Independent.** “It is inclusive. It is welcoming. And it is centered around those who love America.”  
  
**[Gabbard and Kennedy spoke on President Trump’s plan and proposed policies for a commonsense economy (Clip 1)](https://www.tulsigabbard.com/r?u=pXZ4rb398LWXFA59dMExq6-9iecRf8knTMIcEGWzIPuodrH-lWpoyLo1yH2LvKWcQGX4ua2LtohIeqhpCWe4hA&e=c63b506aaa2c5087894f229a9abc7635&utm_source=tulsigabbard&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=20240924_pressrelease&n=3&test_email=1)** to address the country’s sky-high cost of living, the price of groceries, gasoline, housing, and childcare – all of which were substantially lower while Trump served as president. They also covered his plan to stop taxing tips, support small businesses, and bring relief to working-class Americans who are struggling under the current economic climate.

Gabbard and Kennedy explained how under President Trump, the United States had no new wars, but over the past three years, the country has seen great unrest with the invasion of Ukraine, wars in the Middle East, and the terrorist actions of Hamas – with no end in sight under Vice President Kamala Harris. **[Gabbard then shared how she was recently put on a secret domestic terrorist watchlist as political retaliation for stating that Kamala Harris is dangerously unfit to serve as commander in chief (Clip 2).](https://www.tulsigabbard.com/r?u=pXZ4rb398LWXFA59dMExq-ZohQ_6HUVJOdmfCii6AHSnqT0jhKp45tKCGjk7TIvkptdKqO0wdSQNW5huPfAD_w&e=c63b506aaa2c5087894f229a9abc7635&utm_source=tulsigabbard&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=20240924_pressrelease&n=5&test_email=1)**  
  
“It’s not only meant to bully and intimidate people like me,” **said Tulsi Gabbard**, “but the bigger effect is that it sends a chilling message to anyone who is paying attention, ‘If you speak out against us, if you challenge our power and our policies – and our greed and ambition for more power – then you too will be punished.’ What do we do about this? Keep speaking up. And speaking loudly. We defend our right to free speech by _using_ our right to free speech.”

**About LTC Tulsi Gabbard**

Four-term Congresswoman and 2020 Presidential Candidate Tulsi Gabbard is a combat veteran and Lieutenant Colonel in the U.S. Army Reserve, keynote speaker, and bestselling author of _**[For Love of Country: Leave the Democrat Party Behind](https://www.tulsigabbard.com/r?u=6tVwsfSE9c5CfrOLlkJpd7dZJ3wC9mgHbEc0MdJKwEs&e=c63b506aaa2c5087894f229a9abc7635&utm_source=tulsigabbard&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=20240924_pressrelease&n=7&test_email=1)**_. She was elected to the Hawaii Legislature at the age of twenty-one, served on three deployments to war zones in the Middle East and Africa, and represented Hawaii’s 2nd Congressional District from 2013 to 2021, serving on the Armed Services, Homeland Security, and Foreign Affairs Committees. She was Vice Chair of the Democratic National Committee until stepping down in 2016. Tulsi Gabbard left the Democrat Party in 2022. She endorsed Donald J. Trump for President in 2024 and co-chairs his transition team along with Robert F. Kennedy Jr.

**About Robert F. Kennedy Jr.**

Robert F. Kennedy Jr.'s career of public service began in 1985 as an attorney for the environmental nonprofit RiverKeeper. He eventually became one of the most influential environmentalists in the United States, receiving TIME Magazine's "Hero of the Planet” and the Sartisky Peace Award. He has spent nearly 40 years fighting corrupt corporations and government agencies. Bobby Kennedy Jr. comes from an illustrious political family. The son of Senator Robert F. Kennedy and the nephew of President John F. Kennedy, Bobby Jr. was a lifelong Democrat but became increasingly estranged from the party in the 2010s as it drifted away from its traditional values. He made his final break on October 9, 2023, when he announced his candidacy as an independent for President of the United States. Ten months later, he endorsed Donald J. Trump for President and co-chairs his transition team along with Tulsi Gabbard.  

**About Danica Patrick**

As a racecar driver, Danica Patrick broke barriers and set records with her on-track performance, succeeding in the male-dominated world of professional motorsports. Moving into her next chapter, retired from racing, Danica’s focus has shifted to her role as an entrepreneur and business owner. Danica has launched the clothing line – Warrior by Danica Patrick, authored a book Pretty Intense, and is the sole proprietor of Somnium, a vineyard in Napa Valley, California. In addition to these passion projects, Danica also continues to do inspirational speaking engagements, and she plans to build upon these initiatives in the coming years.
