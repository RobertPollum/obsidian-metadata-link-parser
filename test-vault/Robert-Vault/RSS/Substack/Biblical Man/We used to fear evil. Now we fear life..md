We used to have three monkeys.

See no evil.

Hear no evil.

Speak no evil.

Now a fourth one has emerged.

He sees no one.

He hears no one.

He speaks to no one.

He just scrolls.

And men call this “rest.”

They call it “decompression.”

They call it “me time.”

They call it “privacy.”

But it’s not rest.

It’s retreat.

It’s digital hermitage—men hiding from the responsibilities…
