A few weeks ago, a man got on the New York subway wearing his expensive Meta AI glasses. They cost $300, but give users super powers—for example [identifying total strangers](https://www.cdpinstitute.org/news/meta-smart-glasses-can-id-strangers-in-seconds/), and taking [quasi-surveillance videos](https://gizmodo.com/meta-is-turning-its-ray-bans-into-a-surveillance-machine-for-ai-2000596395).

It’s a step below Superman. But it’s the best that Mark Zuckerberg can offer right now.

A woman on the subway wasn’t impressed. She stepped up to this tech-loving bro, and grabbed his glasses. Then she broke them in two with her bare hands.

And he called her a “Karen”—which seems to be the new way of saying _bitch_.
