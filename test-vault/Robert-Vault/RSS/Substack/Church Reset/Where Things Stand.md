As of January 3, I will have completed 15 years in this ministry of writing, speaking, and podcasting at Focus Press.

It’s been an incredible ride thus far, with 12 books published, well over 500 articles written, 200+ podcast episodes, Gospel meetings in 12 states and 2 foreign countries, 3 website builds, and now 3 years and over 4,000 followers accumulated on Substack. God has blessed me greatly with the opportunity to do this work and meet so many wonderful people in the process.

Truth be told, though, it’s entirely possible that 2025 was my final year doing this work full-time.

That’s not a gimmicky fundraising ploy. I have job applications out in secular work and if the right one comes in, I’m going to have to take it. I’d love to raise enough funds to make this work self-sustaining regardless of whether Focus Press can continue to meet my salary, but I can’t guarantee that will happen.

Lord willing, I will always continue to write here at least weekly as it is my greatest passion, but the production elsewhere will certainly fall off should I have to change careers.

My work load for this year alone:

-   Over 150 articles, producing ~500,000 views
    
-   The writing and production of the 5-star rated _[You Are Saved: the Christian’s Assurance](https://amzn.to/3YgnOmR)_
    
-   The co-writing, editing, and production of 4 A Closer Walk with Thee 90-day [devotional volumes](https://amzn.to/3MV7qWw)
    
-   The re-release of J.W. McGarvey’s classic _A Treatise on the Eldership_
    
-   47 episodes of _Think Deeper Podcast,_ with 46 accompanying _Deep End_ exclusive episodes
    
-   A read-through and revision [video series on](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbudyT-_0aYWxxrksvFHzD2003Av1xbyj) _[Church Reset](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbudyT-_0aYWxxrksvFHzD2003Av1xbyj)_ for the book’s 5 year anniversary
    
-   The public release of 22 _[Scriptural Focus](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbudyT-_0aYVKlrWvBgGIMVqWpT9nQQMC)_ Q&A videos
    
-   4 [Cultural Breakdown](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbudyT-_0aYVR6y8OukQF26sfsEVlghjx) episodes
    
-   Teaching series through Romans and now the Minor Prophets for [Focus+](http://patreon.com/focuspressinc)
    
-   2 Gospel meetings along with dozens of other preaching and teaching sessions
    
-   Guest spots on video podcasts _Godly Young Men_, _In Between Sundays_, _The Rasor’s Edge_, and _Cogitations_
    

I have more big projects I hope to complete in 2026, including two new books, 4 more _Closer Walk_ devotionals, a video/podcast series on Revelation, the long-awaited return of the _Who Let the Dogma Out?_ theology podcast, and more. But, it’s all up in the air right now.

If God provides the means to continue to do it full-time, I’ll be thrilled to keep going. If He wants me to explore another opportunity, then that’s what I’ll do.

In any case, I thank you for reading, for subscribing, for supporting, and I pray you have a blessed new year.
