Laila Cunningham is a British politician, former senior Crown Prosecution Service prosecutor, and current Westminster City Councillor for Lancaster Gate. Born in London to Egyptian immigrant parents, she is a practising Muslim, mother of seven, and defected from the Conservatives to Reform UK in 2025. In January 2026, Reform UK named her as their candidate for the 2028 London mayoral election, focusing on tough crime policies, controlled immigration, and British values.

On Monday, she is joining Francis and Konstantin in the studio to discuss her story, why she is running for London Mayor, Sadiq Khan, crime in London and much more.
