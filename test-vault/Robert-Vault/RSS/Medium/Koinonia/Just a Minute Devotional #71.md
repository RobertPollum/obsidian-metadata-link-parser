#### A clean-slated new day

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/0*8pqSUIBr7o_JOkSE)

Photo by [Elena Rabkina](https://unsplash.com/@rabkina?utm_source=medium&utm_medium=referral) on [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&utm_medium=referral)

> The steadfast love of the Lord never ceases; his mercies never come to an end; they are new every morning; great is your faithfulness. (Lamentations 3:22–23, NRSV)

Many mornings, I sit on my porch and watch as the sun begins to light the night sky. I wait and watch with wonder His miracle of precision; the Earth rotating, bringing the light of the sun on the morning horizon.

He is faithful in every way, and I contemplate another new day that the Lord has made and placed in time as a granted favor.

I see the clean slate before me that I have not yet tarnished or marred. I am also aware of the gravity of my responsibility for the actions I take each second, minute, and hour, which will shape my biography of tomorrow.

I stand amazed in the Lord’s love and His continual mercies that are new every day. He has freely handed me a Deed of Full Reconveyance for which I am unworthy. My sin debt is paid in full, and the lien removed.

I will stand before God blamelessly made Holy by the blood of Jesus Christ. So not only does He give me a clean-slated day, but also a clean-slated heart. It is nothing I have done, but what He has done for me out of His steadfast love.

For this reason, each new day I want to give my gift of complete devotion as a sacrifice of praise before His altar. I want to be an example of love and adoration to Him. I want my witness to be a beam of His light, piercing through the darkness of pain in this world.

I want to be His obedient laborer. I want to shower others with the same measure of mercy He has shown to me. I want to weigh my words, speak His truth through loving lips, and convey His story.

#### Prayer:

_Lord, help me not leave too many scars on the 365 days you afford me every year. I will honor and cherish your gift of each clean-slated new day. You are love, dearest One, so help me to love just like You. Let my biography show your great faithfulness!_
