Draw near to God and He will draw near to you. (James 4:8 NKJV) This message in James is one of my favorite Bible verses. It has constantly reminded me of the importance of drawing near to and spending time with God. The second half of the verse shows us that He is the one we should draw near to or come to when we have unconfessed sin in our lives. God desires to spend time with us, not just when things are good, but also when we struggle, whether with sin or with disappointing life...
